Why STUPA Sport Analytics?

Stupa Sports analysis help coaches to monitor and improve the performance of their players and teams. Stupa demonstrates your strengths and weaknesses. Our purpose is to find key analysis points.

  • Players & Coaches Demand more Data, Faster with less effort! – That’s why we launched STUPA, to save your time. The level of competition and the importance of data-driven decisions are higher than ever. The burden of this on coaches is real and has pushed us to evolve the role. Stupa Sports Analysis plays an important role in realizing goals and producing long term results – that’s why professional coaches rely on it!


  • Reports generated from Stupa sports analytics can be used to understand the opposing opponent’s game plans and strategies. If you understand your opposition’s strengths you can put effective strategies, if you understand their weaknesses you can coach your player accordingly.
